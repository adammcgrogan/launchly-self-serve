Switching your design

You can move your site to any of our designs at any time — your content, images, and settings carry over automatically. Nothing is lost when you switch.

How to switch

Go to Dashboard → your site → Switch design, pick a design, and confirm. Your live site updates immediately.

What to check afterwards

Different designs emphasise content differently — after switching, it's worth reviewing your Appearance settings (colours and heading font) to make sure they still suit the new design.
