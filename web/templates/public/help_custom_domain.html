Connecting a custom domain

Connecting your own domain (e.g. yourbusiness.com) is a Pro-plan feature. It replaces your launchly.ltd subdomain as your site's main address.

1. Add your domain

Go to Dashboard → your site → Custom domain, and enter the domain you own. You don't need the "www" prefix.

2. Add DNS records at your registrar

Once you've added your domain, we show you the DNS records to create — a CNAME record pointing your domain at Launchly, plus verification records. Add these in the DNS settings of whoever you registered your domain with (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.), not in Launchly.

3. Wait for verification

DNS changes can take anywhere from a few minutes to a few hours to take effect. Your domain's status starts as "pending" and moves to "active" automatically once we can see the records. Use the Check status button on the domain page any time to re-check without waiting for the automatic refresh.

If a domain gets stuck or shows "failed", double-check the records match exactly what's shown on the domain page — a stray "www" or missing record is the most common cause.

Removing a domain

Click Remove domain on the domain page. Your site immediately falls back to its launchly.ltd subdomain.
