Changing colours and fonts

Each design comes with a handful of colour palettes and heading fonts you can switch between without touching your content.

How to change them

Go to Dashboard → your site → Appearance. Pick a colour palette and a heading font, then save — your live site updates immediately.

Heading font options

Choose "Design default" to use whatever font your current design ships with, or override it with a sans-serif or serif heading font.
