Changing your subdomain

Every site gets a free address on launchly.ltd, e.g. yourbusiness.launchly.ltd. You can change the part before ".launchly.ltd" at any time.

How to change it

Go to Dashboard → your site → Address, enter the new subdomain, and save. It needs to be unique across all Launchly sites.

What happens to your old link

Your previous subdomain keeps working — visitors are redirected to the new one automatically, so links you've already shared or printed won't break.

Limits

You can change your subdomain once per day. If you're on the Pro plan with a custom domain connected, that domain remains your site's main address regardless of the subdomain.
